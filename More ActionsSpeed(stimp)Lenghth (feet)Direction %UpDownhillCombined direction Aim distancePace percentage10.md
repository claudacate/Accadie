For green speed of 10, calculation of aim distance is (number of pace x 2 ) -1 for 1% side slope, if 2% then times the answer by 2, 3% by 3, 0.1% by 0.1, etc.   If downhill then for every 1% add 5% of the above calculation, minus 5% for uphill.   For every increase of green speed add 10%.  eg Green speed of 11, add 10% of aim distance
